Story

The quintessential 90s kid who grew up on dial-up and Super Nintendo's, I've seen the web evolve from its humble beginnings to what it has become today: a ubiquitous, essential part of our lives. The ability to positively influence people through what I create on such a massive scale is what drew me to become a web developer.

I'm fascinated by what this technology is capable of and have dedicated my life to learning its intricacies. My designs focus on simplicity and elegance to provide a coherent experience to all users from all walks of life.

I am up to date on industry standards and am knowledgeable in the full-stack: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript(JQUERY), Ruby on Rails, PHP and SQL. Please see my portfolio at miamiwebgurus.com, my resume on linkedin, or go ahead and shoot me an email!
